Theme

“Scopas and the Battle of the Floyera” teaches readers that they should not claim to be equal with gods who are clearly the wiser. The theme is shown in the exposition because Scopas says that he is better than everyone even the gods. The theme is also shown in the rising action because Scopas was challenged by Apollo which leads to him getting thrown into prison. Again the theme is shown in the climax because Scopas gets thrown into prison for challenging Apollo and breaking the Sun Chariot. In addition, the theme is shown in the falling action because Scopas rots in prison because of his bragging, Finally, the theme is shown in the resolution because Scopas died in prison because of his bragging.
